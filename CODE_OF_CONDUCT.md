# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Team Harness adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1, as its Code of Conduct. The complete text is published at
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/ and is
incorporated here by reference.

In short: this project is committed to a welcoming, professional, and
harassment-free experience for everyone who participates, regardless of
background or identity. Participants are expected to act respectfully; conduct
that demeans, intimidates, or excludes others is not acceptable.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — the repository, issues,
pull requests, and any other official channel — and also when an individual is
representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Concerns about unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer at
**mgutiers3012@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly. The maintainer will respect the privacy and security of
the reporter.

Enforcement follows the Contributor Covenant 2.1 Enforcement Guidelines
(Correction, Warning, Temporary Ban, Permanent Ban) — see the full text linked
above for the details of each tier.
